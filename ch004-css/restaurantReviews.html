Marnic's

Beach Road
Lordship, Stratford, CT

The Review

Marnic's is a great little diner attached to Marnic's Inn. It sits right on the water in the Lordship section of Stratford, CT

Marnic's Highlights

  • Great views of the water
  • Simple yet delicious and reasonably priced dishes
  • Great views of the water
  • Soft ice cream stand
